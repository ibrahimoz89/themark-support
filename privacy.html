Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 17, 2025

Your privacy matters. THE MARK is designed with privacy at its core. We collect minimal data and never sell your information to third parties.

1. Information We Collect

THE MARK collects the following information to provide and improve our service:

Phone Number:

  • Used solely for authentication and account security
  • Required to prevent spam and ensure one mark per day per user
  • Never shared with third parties or used for marketing

User-Generated Content:

  • The text marks you create and place on the canvas
  • Visible to all users on the public canvas
  • Subject to our content moderation policies

Technical Data:

  • Device information for app functionality and crash prevention
  • Anonymous usage analytics to improve the app experience
  • No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information exclusively for:

  • Authenticating users and maintaining account security
  • Enforcing the one-mark-per-day limit
  • Displaying user marks on the public canvas
  • Content moderation to maintain a safe community
  • Improving app performance and user experience
  • Preventing fraud and spam

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption:

  • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Stored on secure Firebase servers with enterprise-grade security
  • Access is strictly limited to essential operations only
  • Regular security audits and updates

4. Anonymity

THE MARK is designed to be anonymous:

  • No profiles, usernames, or public identities
  • Marks are not linked to your phone number publicly
  • No social graph or follower systems
  • Your identity remains private

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Data is only shared in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Firebase (Google) for backend services, under their privacy policy
  • When required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • To prevent fraud or ensure platform security

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Access the data we have collected about you
  • Opt out of non-essential data collection
  • Contact us with privacy concerns or questions

7. Children's Privacy

THE MARK is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

8. International Users

THE MARK is available worldwide. By using the app, you consent to the transfer and processing of your data in accordance with this privacy policy, regardless of your location.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app. Your continued use of THE MARK after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Third-Party Services

THE MARK uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, and analytics

These services have their own privacy policies governing data use.
